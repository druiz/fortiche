Paste any workout program — Fortiche turns it into a plan you can train with.

Copy a program from your coach, a forum, a PDF, or your notes, paste it into Fortiche, and on-device Apple Intelligence turns it into a structured, trackable plan: days, exercises, sets, reps, weights, rest times. "Bench 3x5 @ 80kg", "OHP 3×8–12", "5/3/1", AMRAP, RPE, %1RM — it understands how lifters actually write programs. Nothing you paste ever leaves your device.

BUILT FOR THE GYM FLOOR
One giant button per set. Big steppers for quick weight and rep changes. No fiddly gestures, no tiny targets — it works with sweaty hands, gloves, and chalk. A rest timer starts itself when you finish a set, with haptics on your wrist and a countdown on your lock screen.

YOUR WRIST OR YOUR PHONE — OR BOTH
Train from Apple Watch with heart rate live on screen: turn the Digital Crown to adjust weight, Double Tap to log a set. Your iPhone mirrors the whole workout live — change weight, reps, or sets on whichever device is closer, and both stay in sync. No watch? The full experience works on iPhone alone.

LIVE ACTIVITY THAT LIFTS WITH YOU
Your current set, prescription, and rest countdown live in the Dynamic Island and on your Lock Screen — with buttons to log a set, skip rest, or pause without unlocking your phone.

HANDS FULL? ASK SIRI.
"Start my push day." "Log 8 reps at 80 kilos." "Skip my rest." "Next exercise." Deep App Intents integration puts your whole workout a sentence away — also via the Action button and Shortcuts.

KNOW YOU'RE PROGRESSING
Every finished workout is saved with personal records (estimated 1RM), weekly volume charts, and a "last time" reminder on every set so you always know what to beat. Workouts count toward your Activity rings via Apple Health.

AND THE DETAILS THAT MATTER
• Programs are fully editable — tweak sets by hand or re-parse from the original text
• Your next training day is suggested automatically, based on what you did last
• Plate calculator (kg and lb) so you never do bar math mid-set
• A public-domain library of 870+ exercises, matched even when you write "OHP" or "RDLs"
• Pick your app icon: five colorways
• kg/lb, your choice

PRIVATE BY ARCHITECTURE
No account. No server. No analytics. Programs and history live on your devices and in your private iCloud; workouts save to Apple Health under your control. The AI runs entirely on-device.

Fortiche requires iOS 27 and is built for Apple Intelligence. Program parsing uses the on-device model where available, with a fast built-in parser everywhere else.
