# Fortiche Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 2026_

Fortiche is built so that your data never reaches us — because there is no "us"
to reach. The app has no server, no accounts, and no analytics.

## What the app stores, and where

- **Workout programs and history** are stored on your device and, if iCloud is
  enabled, synced through your **private iCloud database** (CloudKit). Only
  your Apple Account can access it; the developer cannot.
- **Workouts, heart rate, and body mass** are read from and saved to
  **Apple Health** only with your permission, and remain under your control in
  the Health app.
- **Program text you paste** is parsed **entirely on-device** by Apple
  Intelligence (or a built-in parser). It is never uploaded.

## What the app sends over the network

Nothing, with one optional exception: exercise photos in the exercise detail
view are fetched from the public [free-exercise-db](https://github.com/yuhonas/free-exercise-db)
repository on GitHub's CDN. That request contains no personal data.

## What we collect

Nothing. No identifiers, no usage analytics, no crash reporting, no tracking.
